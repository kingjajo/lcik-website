Who We Are

The Liberian Community in Kenya (LCIK) is a non-profit, social and cultural organization established to unite and support all Liberians living in Kenya. We serve as a platform to celebrate our culture, uplift our members, and foster strong relationships with the broader Kenyan society.

Our Mission

To promote unity, empower our members, and preserve our rich Liberian heritage while building a strong, supportive community abroad..

Our Vision

To be a united and empowered Liberian diaspora in Kenya, fostering cultural pride, mutual support, and impactful collaboration for the prosperity and well-being of all members.
